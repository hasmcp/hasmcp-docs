---
title: "Licenses"
description: "HasMCP has 3 product types and 2 licences."
---

HasMCP has three product types and two different licences. Pro and Enterprise
versions are exempt from the restrictions of AGPL v3 or later versions when they
have active valid license.

| Product                              | License            |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------ |
| HasMCP Community Edition (hasmcp-ce) | AGPL v3            |
| HasMCP Pro (hasmcp-pro)              | Commercial License |
| HasMCP Enterprise (hasmcp)           | Commercial License |

For queries and licensing, please send email to license@hasmcp.com.
